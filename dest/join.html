Join Us!
We are hiring at all career stages.

We are looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team and lead the next generation of synthetic biology and cell engineering. We are looking for predoctoral staff, graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and PhD scientists.

Research Technicians (Predoctoral)

Research technicians are an essential part of our team. Past members have gone on to PhD, MD, and industry positions including at UCSF, Harvard, Lyell, Sonoma Bio, Genentech, ArsenalBio, and more.

We typically open recruitment in the fall for positions starting in the summer. Please check back then for more information.

Postdoctoral and Clinical Fellows

Inquiries about postdoctoral position should be emailed to Wendell Lim (limpostdoc@ucsf.edu). Please include three letters of reference and include applicant's name in the subject line.

PhD Students

Motivated graduate students should apply to one of the UCSF graduate programs, such as:

  • •   UCSF TETRAD
  • •   UCSF Biophysics
  • •   UCSF Bioinformatics
  • •   UCSF/Berkeley Bioengineering Program
  • •   UCSF Pharmaceutical Sciences and Pharmacogenomics
  • •   UCSF Chemistry and Chemical Biology.
  • •   UCSF Developmental & Stem Cell Biology.

MS Students

We welcome Master's students through the joint CDI-UCSF Master's program through San Francisco State University.

Undergraduate Students

Motivated undergraduate students should apply to the UCSF Amgen Scholars Program or UCSF Summer Research Training Program (SRTP).
